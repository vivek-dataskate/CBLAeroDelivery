# Product Brief: CBLAero Talent Sourcing Platform

**Category:** Recruitment Technology / Aviation Staffing

**Target Market:**
- Aviation staffing agencies, regional airlines, aircraft MROs, and maintenance organizations.
- Small-to-medium sized firms seeking automation and cost savings.

**Problem Statement:**
Aviation recruiters currently rely on generic applicant tracking systems that lack domain-specific intelligence, resulting in lengthy sourcing cycles, high labour costs, and poor candidate fit. Handling certifications, FAA compliance, and candidate availability is manual and error-prone.

**Solution:**
CBLAero delivers a SaaS platform that automates candidate discovery, enrichment, engagement, and availability tracking specific to aviation roles. The system uses AI-driven match scoring, multi-channel outreach (SMS and email), and real-time data to surface top candidates in minutes, not days.

**Key Features:**
- Domain-aware database with FAA certifications & type ratings.
- Multi-agent architecture orchestrating ingestion, enrichment, matching, and outreach.
- Deduplication and confidence scoring engine.
- SMS/email campaigns with A/B testing and compliance.
- Microsoft Teams integration for alerts and recruiter collaboration.
- GDPR/CCPA/TCPA compliance baked in.
- Metrics dashboard (matches/day, CPL, performance).

**Differentiators:**
- Deep aviation focus with built-in regulatory checks.
- Cost-effective pricing (≈$40–50/user/month) vs generic ATS at $150–200.
- Real-time availability tracking and automated communication.
- Native SMS capabilities rather than third-party add-ons.

**Business Model:**
Subscription-based (per recruiter) with optional add-ons for background check integration, premium support, and aviation data feeds.

**Go-to-Market Strategy:**
- Partnerships with aviation training schools and MRO networks.
- Direct sales to staffing agencies and airlines via targeted demos.
- Industry trade shows and online advertising in aviation job boards.

**Success Metrics (Year 1):**
- 100 paying accounts by Q4.
- Reduce average sourcing time to <24 hours.
- Maintain candidate engagement response rate >30%.

**Next Steps:**
Finalize MVP scope (Weeks 1–4 releases), build prototype, gather pilot customer feedback, and iterate on domain features.
